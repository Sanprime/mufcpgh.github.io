The Origins of the Manchester United Supporters Club in Pittsburgh, Morningside

The Manchester United Supporters Club in Pittsburgh, Morningside, was born out of a shared passion for football and a love for one of the most iconic clubs in the world. In the midst of Covid-19 and getting back to a slight sense of normality, a small group of Manchester United fans in Pittsburgh began gathering at The Bulldog Pub to watch matches.

In 2022, Sean the current president of our supporters club took the initiative to establish the Manchester United Supporters Club of Pittsburgh by 2023. The club quickly gained recognition from Manchester United itself, becoming an official Supporters Club.

Today, the Manchester United Supporters Club in Morningside is a vibrant and diverse community of fans from all around the world supporting this legendary club.
